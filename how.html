How It Works

In less than 5 minutes, you can get started selling your digital products and services for free.

It’s Simple and Easy to Set Up

1

Sign up and set up your payouts

Create your Mega Mart account in minutes and add your bank details. Choose how you want to get paid and set your preferred currencies for selling locally or globally.

2

Create and upload your products

Add your products to Mega Mart by uploading your files, setting prices, and filling in product details. Customize your product page and link to match your brand.

3

Share your link and start selling

Share your store or product link anywhere you have an audience. After a purchase, Mega Mart automatically gives the buyer access to your product and handles your payout.
